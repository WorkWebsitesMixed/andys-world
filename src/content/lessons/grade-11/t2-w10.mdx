This is the last design week before you start **making**. Today you assemble your
whole folder, check it all hangs together, and confirm you could walk into the
makerspace and start cutting on day one of Term 3.

<Callout type="exam" title="Why this matters" id="why">
  This completes <ExamLink to="project">Criterion 5</ExamLink> and consolidates your
  whole design folder (C1–C5). A coherent, consistent folder is what makes the
  making phase go smoothly — and scores well.
</Callout>

<KeyTerms terms={[
  { term: "Folder coherence", definition: "Each stage flows from the one before: need → spec → ideas → development → plan." },
  { term: "Consistency check", definition: "Making sure your drawing, production plan and cutting list all agree." },
  { term: "Making-ready", definition: "Everything is in place to start manufacturing with no gaps." },
]} />

<Section title="The lesson" icon="📋" id="lesson">

### 1. Does your folder tell one story?

Read it end to end. Each stage should **flow from the last**: the need leads to the
spec, the spec to the ideas, the ideas to the development, the development to the
plan. Fix anything that jumps or contradicts.

### 2. The consistency check

The classic trap: your **working drawing**, **production plan** and **cutting list**
disagree — a dimension on the drawing doesn't match the cutting list. Cross-check
them now, on paper, where it's cheap to fix.

### 3. Are you making-ready?

The test: could the **technician schedule you**, and could **you start cutting on
day one** of Term 3, from this folder alone?

</Section>

<WorkedExample title="Worked example — a consistency check that saves a part">
1. **Compare** the working drawing with the cutting list.
2. **Spot it:** the drawing says the upright is 300 mm, the cutting list says 280 mm.
3. **Decide** the correct value from your spec and tests (300 mm).
4. **Fix both** documents so they agree — *before* you cut, not after.
</WorkedExample>

<Activity title="Assemble and check" time="55 min + reflection" grouping="Individual (HOD + drawing colleague advise)" materials={["All your folder pages", "Folder-order checklist", "Consistency-check sheet"]}>
1. **Assemble and order** your folder, C1–C5.
2. **Run the consistency check** across your drawing, plan and cutting list.
3. **Fix any gaps**, then set a **making-phase goal** for Term 3.
</Activity>

<Support title="Need a hand? Two checklists">
Use the **folder-order checklist** to get the pages in the right sequence, and the
**consistency-check sheet** to compare drawing ↔ plan ↔ cutting list line by line.
</Support>
