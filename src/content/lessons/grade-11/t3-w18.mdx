Fourth open project session. The urgency should be increasing. If any criterion is still significantly below your target, this session is critical.

<Callout type="exam" title="Why this matters" id="why">
  <ExamLink to="project">Component 2</ExamLink> is worth **50% of your IGCSE**. Two catch-up sessions remain after this one. Every session matters.
</Callout>

<Section title="How to use this session" icon="🔧" id="session">

### Emergency priority list

If you are significantly behind on any of the following, this session addresses that one item only:

**C6 making log:** Every session from T3 W2 to T3 W8 must have an entry. If any are missing, write them now — even from memory.

**C7 evaluation:** Must include: test results (at least 3 spec points tested with measurements), strengths (at least 2 linked to test evidence), weaknesses (at least 2 specific and named), improvements (specific, not generic).

**Photographs:** At least five photographs required (front, side, detail joint, detail circuit, in-use). If any are missing, set up and shoot now.

**Working drawings:** If no working drawings exist in the folder, add at least one A4 dimensioned sketch of the product with at least two views.

### One task, full focus

Choose one item from the emergency list. Work on it for 40 minutes. Do not context-switch.

</Section>
