<Callout type="exam" title="Why this matters" id="why">

<Section title="How to use this session" icon="🔧" id="session">

### Final 45 minutes

**0–30 min:** Complete the B10 task from your B9 progress note.

**30–40 min:** Final folder read-through. Check:
- All seven criteria sections present
- Every C6 making log entry has a date and a photograph
- C7 has test results, strengths, weaknesses, and improvements
- C5 working drawings all have title blocks and third-angle symbols
- Photographs are labelled with captions

**40–45 min:** Write a final note to yourself on the inside cover of the folder: "My strongest criterion is ___. My weakest criterion is ___. For next time, I would ___."

Submit the folder.
