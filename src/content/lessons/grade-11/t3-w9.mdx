This is it — you bring everything together into one **submission-ready folder.** A
year of work, ordered and evidenced from need to evaluation. Your job today is to make
sure **every criterion is clearly shown**, with nothing missing.

<Callout type="exam" title="Why this matters" id="why">
  This is the whole <ExamLink to="project">Component 2</ExamLink> submission — all seven
  criteria (C1–C7). A **moderator** marks from your folder and photographs, so coherence
  and complete evidence are what carry your marks.
</Callout>

<Section title="The lesson" icon="📋" id="lesson">

### 1. Map every criterion

Use a **C1–C7 evidence map**: for each criterion, point to the page(s) that prove it.
If a box is empty, that's a gap to fix today — a missing testing photo, an unfinished
evaluation.

### 2. Coherence and presentation

Order the folder so it reads as **one story**: need → spec → ideas → development →
plan → realisation → evaluation. Tidy, labelled, easy to follow.

### 3. See it as the moderator does

Remember the moderator **never sees your product** — only the folder and photos. Ask:
does this folder *prove* I designed, made and tested a working product for a real user?

</Section>

<WorkedExample title="Worked example — an evidence check that catches a gap">
1. **Go criterion by criterion:** C1 need ✓, C2 spec ✓, C3 ideas ✓, C4 development ✓, C5 plan ✓…
2. **C6 realisation:** overall photo ✓, in-use photo ✓ — good.
3. **C7 testing:** evaluation ✓, but the **testing photo is missing.**
4. **Fix it:** add the load-test photo before submission — gap closed.
</WorkedExample>

<Activity title="Assemble and submit" time="Full session + reflection" grouping="Individual (HOD + colleague advise)" materials={["All folder pages & photographs", "Submission checklist"]}>
1. **Assemble and order** your full folder, C1–C7.
2. **Run the evidence check** — is every criterion clearly shown?
3. **Fix any gaps**, then **reflect**: name two skills you've grown and one to keep
   building.
</Activity>
