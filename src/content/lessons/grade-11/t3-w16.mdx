Another open project session. Use your progress note from B5 to decide where to pick up. If your B5 priority task is complete, identify the next highest-impact task and move to it.

<Callout type="exam" title="Why this matters" id="why">
  <ExamLink to="project">Component 2</ExamLink> is worth **50% of your IGCSE**. Each session you use productively is additional evidence for your folder.
</Callout>

]} />

<Section title="How to use this session" icon="🔧" id="session">

### Starting from your B5 progress note

1. Read your B5 progress note: what did you achieve last session? What remains?
2. Is the B5 priority task complete? If yes, apply the same impact-prioritisation process to the next criterion.
3. If the B5 task is not complete, continue it.

### Folder status check — 2 minutes

Quickly scan your folder. By this session you should have:
- C3, C4, C5 sections complete and filed
- C6 making log with entries for T3 W2–W8 and B5
- Photographs in folder
- C7 evaluation at least in bullet-point draft

If any of the above is missing, that is your priority.

</Section>

1. Read your B5 progress note.
2. Write today's priority task.

**Work (3–40 min):**
3. Work on the priority task.

**Progress note (40–45 min):**
4. Write what was achieved today, what remains.
