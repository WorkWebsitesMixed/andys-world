<Section title="The lesson" icon="📋" id="lesson">

### 1. Safety first — always

Wear the right **PPE**, know where the **emergency stops** are, and use each machine
only as you've been shown. If you're unsure, **ask the technician before acting** —
never guess with a machine.

### 2. Share the space: the staggered rotation

Sixty students can't all be at the laser cutter at once. Follow the **staggered
rotation**: while some are at machines, others are marking out, assembling or
finishing. Be where the schedule says, and free the machine promptly.

### 3. Work to your plan

Open your **production plan** and make parts **in order**. Build the weekly **QC
habit**: check each part against your cutting list and drawing before you move on.
**Book machine time** rather than queuing and waiting.

</Section>

<WorkedExample title="Worked example — a part from file to finished">
1. **Check** your cut/print file is the right part and size.
2. **Set up** the machine (material, settings) with the technician.
3. **Run** the job — staying with it, never leaving it unattended.
4. **QC it:** measure the finished part against your cutting list. Right? Move on. Wrong? Fix before cutting more.
</WorkedExample>

<Activity title="Safety check, then make" time="Full session (technician-managed rotation)" grouping="Staggered stations" materials={["Your production plan & cut files", "PPE", "Stock materials"]}>
1. **Pass the makerspace safety check.**
2. **Start manufacturing your first part** following your plan, under supervision.
3. **Log your progress** against your production plan before you tidy up.
</Activity>

<Support title="Need a hand? Stations, buddies and pre-checked files">
Work at the **technician-led stations** and use the **buddy system** — two pairs of
eyes are safer. If CAD was tricky, your files have been **pre-checked**, so you can
focus on making.
</Support>
